Houston

Space City
Slogan: The City With No Limits

Houston is the largest city in Texas. Positioned near the Gulf of Mexico, it's a major hub for the energy industry, particularly oil and natural gas, with the presence of many Fortune 500 energy companies. Houston is also home to the Texas Medical Center, the largest medical complex in the world, and NASA's Johnson Space Center, where mission control for space flights is located. The city has a vibrant cultural scene, with a strong commitment to the arts displayed through various museums and theaters, including the Houston Museum of Natural Science and the Houston Grand Opera. Its diverse population has cultivated a rich culinary scene with a wide array of international cuisines. As a testament to its sports tradition, Houston hosts several major professional sports teams such as the Houston Astros in MLB and the Houston Texans in the NFL. The blend of cutting-edge industry, arts, cuisine, and sports creates a dynamic environment that draws people from around the world to this Texas metropolis. Learn more on Wikipedia!
