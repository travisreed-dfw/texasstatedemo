Dallas

Big-D
Slogan: Find Your All.

Dallas is one of the largest and most vibrant cities in the state of Texas, situated in the northern part of the state. As a key hub for commerce, culture, and technology, Dallas is home to various Fortune 500 companies and has a diverse economy driven by sectors such as finance, information technology, healthcare, and telecommunications. The city is renowned for its arts scene, boasting a plethora of museums, galleries, and performance venues like the Dallas Museum of Art and the AT&T Performing Arts Center. Sports enthusiasts can find a strong presence of professional teams, including the Dallas Cowboys in the NFL and the Dallas Mavericks in the NBA. Dallas is also famous for its culinary delights, with a wide array of dining options that reflect the city's rich cultural diversity. Furthermore, the city played a significant role in the history of the United States, as it was the site of President John F. Kennedy's assassination in 1963, an event that is memorialized at the Sixth Floor Museum at Dealey Plaza. Learn more on Wikipedia!
